Rookie Year Robot

Our FIRST ever robot

The Rookie Year Robot was the first robot we ever created on our first year.

To pick up blocks and balls we wanted to use an arm with a scoop. We wanted the arm to have a 4 arm linkage so that the end of the arm would always stay parallel to the ground. This way the drivers wouldn't have to worry about the angle of the end of the arm changing. Below is the picture of the arm attached to the tank tread drive.
